# UniRanked

Objective: Help students understand their likelihood of admission to specific universities, based on real, verified data from past applicants.

**User flow (simplified):**

1. A student **submits** their application info (grades, GPA, SAT/ACT, location, major, universities applied to, accepted/rejected list).
2. Data goes into an **anonymous public database**.
3. Other users can **search or filter** (e.g., â€œCS at Stanfordâ€, â€œfrom Indiaâ€, â€œGPA 3.9â€) to see aggregate trends â€” acceptance rates by stat range, geography, etc.
